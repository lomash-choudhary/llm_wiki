# LLM Wiki Core

## Goal

Build a small LLM Wiki that keeps Raw source material separate from compiled Wiki notes, then lets agents maintain a reusable knowledge graph for humans and AI tools.

## Current Notes

- Raw sources should stay untouched as evidence.
- Wiki notes should be clean, structured, interlinked pages compiled from sources.
- The maintenance layer should keep the Wiki accurate by checking coverage, contradictions, outdated information, and orphan pages.
