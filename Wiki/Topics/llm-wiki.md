# LLM Wiki

## Summary

An LLM Wiki is a shared, structured knowledge layer that sits between Raw sources and AI tools. The source describes it as a persistent wiki that an AI agent can build and maintain so knowledge is compiled once, kept current, and reused across tools instead of being rederived on every query.

## Key Points

- Raw sources remain separate from compiled Wiki notes.
- AI agents read Raw sources and turn them into clean, interlinked, structured pages.
- The Wiki can serve both human understanding and agentic AI workflows.
- Maintenance is part of the system: agents can check for contradictions, outdated information, and orphan pages.
