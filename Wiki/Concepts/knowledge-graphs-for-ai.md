# Knowledge Graphs For AI

## Summary

A knowledge graph represents knowledge as connected nodes and edges. The source explains the basic model as nodes, edges, and triples: a subject, a relationship, and an object. This structure can help AI follow relationships between ideas instead of only retrieving isolated text chunks.

## Key Points

- A node is a thing, such as a person, idea, place, event, component, or concept.
- An edge is a named relationship between two nodes.
- A triple combines subject, relationship, and object into a small unit of graph knowledge.
- The source contrasts ordinary RAG with graph-based retrieval for complex questions that depend on relationships between documents.
